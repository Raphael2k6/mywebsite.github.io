About Raphael Donanu

Raphael Donanu was born in the outskirts of Port Harcourt, Rivers state, Nigeria and spent all his childhood and a good part of his adult life in Port Harcourt. He holds a bachelorâ€™s degree in microbiology from the university of Port Harcourt and he is certified in Health Safety and Environment, Fire Safety and Risk Assessment, and Basic First Aid and CPR. Despite his science background, he has a strong track record on leadership having led in various capacities starting from his undergraduate days. He is a member of the Young African Leaders Initiative (YALI Network) and holds certifications in fundamentals of starting and running a business, strategies for personal growth and development, and workforce collaboration and development, all from YALI network.

He is passionate about purposeful leadership and transformation through innovation in African and have been involved in volunteering for NGOs that provide social services to underprivileged rural communities and also served as the director of the youth centre of a Christian organization where he mentored young people and organized empowerment programs aimed that their personal deveolpment self sustainability. He has experiences working as the missions coordinator of a Christian organization where he developed strategy, identified, planned and executed outreach and social impact programs.

He also has experiences in International development working as an intern at an international development research organization where he was involved in template development, collection and analysis of data, and GPS mapping to monitor development projects. He has a good understanding of sustainable development goals, baseline, midterm and endline surveys, monitoring and evaluation, developing RFPs and programme implementation. Due to his strong believe that development in Africa will be driven by innovation, He has skills in programming and web development.

Ministry and Purpose

Raphael Donanu has an assignment to deliver purposeful leadership in every sphere of his endeavor. He is passionate about God and preaching the gospel of Jesus Christ with eveidential demonstration of the power of the Holy Spirit. He is a called teacher of the word with a strong healing ministry. He is the president of The Inspired Church a church arm of his vast ministry and also involved in youth transformation and reconstruction programmes world over and have spoken on several platforms to young people.
